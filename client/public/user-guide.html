ArtSpace (아지트) User Guide

How to find, book, and manage art spaces on ArtSpace (아지트).

🚀 Getting Started

1

Create an Account

Click Login on the top-right, then go to Sign Up. Enter your username and password to create a free account.

2

Sign In

Once registered, sign in with your username and password. Your account badge will appear in the nav.

🔍 Finding a Space

1

Browse Available Spaces

Go to Book Space. Use the filters to narrow by type — Rehearsal, Gallery, Performance Hall, Studio, or Workshop.

2

Check Availability

Click Check Availability on any space card to see the calendar and open time slots.

3

Select Your Date & Time

Pick a date and time slot that works for you. You'll see the cost per hour or per session before confirming.

📋 Making a Booking

1

Confirm Your Booking

Review the space details, date, time, and price. Click Confirm Booking to send your request.

2

Receive Confirmation

You'll receive a booking confirmation by email once the venue approves your request — usually within a few hours.

3

Manage Your Bookings

View all upcoming and past bookings from your account dashboard. Cancel or modify at least 24 hours in advance.

🏛️ Listing Your Space (Venue Owners)

1

Create a Venue Account

Sign up and select I own a space. Upgrade to a Venue plan to list your space on ArtSpace (아지트).

2

Add Your Space

Fill in your space details — name, location, capacity, type, and hourly rate. Add photos to attract more bookings.

3

Manage Bookings & Availability

Set your availability calendar, approve or decline booking requests, and track payouts from your venue dashboard.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it free to book a space?

Creating an account is free. The Basic plan allows up to 3 bookings per month at no cost. See Pricing for more.

How do I cancel a booking?

Go to your dashboard → My Bookings, find the booking, and select Cancel. Cancellations must be made at least 24 hours before the booking time.

Can I book a space for multiple days?

Yes. When selecting availability, you can choose multiple consecutive time slots or submit a multi-day request directly to the venue.

I forgot my password. What do I do?

On the login page, click Forgot password to receive a reset link by email.

How do I list my space?

Sign up, upgrade to a Venue plan from Pricing, and follow the steps in the Listing section above.
